The process

Five simple steps to enrollment

We've made joining Highlands warm, personal, and straightforward.

  1. 1

    Step 01

    Schedule a personal tour

    Walk our halls, meet our teachers, and picture your child here. Tours are relaxed, personal, and the best first step.

  2. 2

    Step 02

    Submit your application

    Complete the secure online application through our FACTS/RenWeb portal. It takes just a few minutes to begin.

  3. 3

    Step 03

    Assessment & family interview

    A friendly assessment and conversation help us understand your child and ensure Highlands is the right fit.

  4. 4

    Step 04

    Enrollment decision

    Receive your decision and, upon acceptance, complete enrollment and registration to secure your child's spot.

  5. 5

    Step 05

    Welcome to the family

    Get your welcome packet, meet your classroom, and step into a community that will know and love your child.
